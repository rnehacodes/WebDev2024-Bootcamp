The Best Movies according to Neha

My 3 Favorite recent movies

HeeraMandi

It is a classic Sanjay Leela Bhansali Movie depicting a captivating and dramatic stories of Queens of HeeraMandi. It is a directorial masterpiece and is a must watch especially for the aesthetics.

Laapata Ladies

Honestly, regret not watching it in theatre but no worries, watching on Netflix was heartwarming too. It is a lovely story of women and made by women. Women's journey of becoming independent and loving herself and admiring her aspirations.

Unlocked

So, this is a south korean thriller that very well explains Samsung's obsession with too much security. Also, it makes the viewer cautious of making their phone secure.
