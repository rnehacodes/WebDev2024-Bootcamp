The Best Movies According to Neha

My top 3 favorite movies of all-time have been:

1. 3 Idiots

A classic Rajkumar Hirani Masterpiece that shooks us our fundamentals about success apparently set by society.

2. Meri Pyari Bindu

A very cute, funny and heartwarming movie that teaches us the importance of letting go yet embracing the memories of people who have been left behind.

3. Sita Ramam

A romantic journey of two characters and their everlasting love.
